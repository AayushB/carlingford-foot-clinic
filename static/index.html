DIABETIC FOOT HEALTH

With regular foot health assessments, proactive treatments, footwear and foot care education many of the diabetic foot complications can be avoided.

Our complete Diabetic foot management includes:

• Neurological and Vascular assessments
• Regular foot care and treatment
• Foot care education
• Wound management
• Biomechanical assessment
• Appropriate footwear advice
• Preventative footwear and orthotic treatment

CORN & CALLUS

Our podiatrist can remove and prevent the reoccurrences of corn and callus.

Corn & callus normally appear on high-pressure areas such as metatarsal heads, heels, overlapping toes and bunions. Heel callus can commonly form fissures, which can breakdown easily causing pain and infection. Depending on the diagnosis a footwear recommendation, simple deflection, orthotics therapy, silipos or otoform devises maybe prescribed for long-term prevention and cushioning.

PLANATAR WART TREATMENT

A plantar wart also known as veruca, is a wart caused by the human papilloma virus (HPV). It normally occurs on the sole of feet and can be a painful. It is a very common foot problem.

We offer a range of treatments to encourage the removal of plantar warts.

ORTHOTIC THERAPY

We perform a detailed biomechanical assessment of your lower limb before prescribing an orthotics. Our aim is to provide long-term solution to acute and chronic foot condition, lower limb and postural correction and to diminish pain.

Orthotics therapy also contributes in treatment and prevention of corns and callouses by redistributing the pressure on feet. A comprehensive follow up and adjustment of orthotics will be performed as per treatment plan.

TAPING

Taping is done by direct application of tape on skin. We perform taping to:

• Reduce soft tissue swelling
• Restrict the motion of injured part
• Protect injured part
• Used as splint
• Enhance the healing process
• Reduce pain
